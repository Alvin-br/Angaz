Angaza was founded in 2023 November but came into full action in January 2025. Founded by Liana Achieng' Rusana and Co founder Joan Wanjohi and a followed recruitmentof 5 officials. It was built on foundation of helping the marginalised in community but mostly to uplift the spirits of disadvantaged kids and give them hopefor the future. It has actively been organising and undertaking heartwarming events since..
